## About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Institute of Biomedical Sciences, Academia Sinica (Taiwan).

My research focuses on **innate immune regulation in the lung**, particularly in the context of **respiratory viral infection** and **asthma**. I use in vivo mouse models, high-dimensional immune profiling, and single-cell transcriptomic approaches to study how innate immune cell networks coordinate immune defense, inflammation, and lung tissue homeostasis.

## What’s on the website

The site is organized into a few core pages:

- **Education & Experiences**: training and appointments
- **Publications**: selected peer-reviewed work
- **Awards & honors**: awards, fellowships, and recognitions
- **Research resources**: links and tools (e.g., the ILC Knowledge Hub)
